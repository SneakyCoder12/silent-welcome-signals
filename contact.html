Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with FinScope?

Getting started with FinScope is easy! Simply click on the "Login" button in the top right corner and create a free account. Once registered, you'll have access to our basic financial tools and data. For more advanced features, you can upgrade to one of our premium plans.

What data sources do you use?

We source our financial data from multiple reliable providers including major stock exchanges, central banks, and financial institutions. All data is validated and cross-referenced to ensure accuracy. Our news content comes from reputable financial news outlets and is updated in real-time.

Is my financial data secure with FinScope?

Absolutely. Security is our top priority. We use bank-level encryption to protect all user data, and we never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent. Our security measures include two-factor authentication, regular security audits, and continuous monitoring for suspicious activities.

Can I use FinScope on mobile devices?

Yes, FinScope is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. Our web application is optimized for mobile use, and we also offer dedicated iOS and Android apps for an enhanced mobile experience with features like push notifications for market alerts.

What are the pricing plans for FinScope?

FinScope offers a free basic plan with limited access to financial data and tools. Our premium plans start at $9.99/month for individual investors, $29.99/month for professional users, and custom enterprise pricing for organizations. All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial, so you can try before you commit.
